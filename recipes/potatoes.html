Fried potatoes

Fried eggs

A very simple dish made of potatoes.

It is very delicious.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (3 or more pieces depending on your needs)
  • Oil(1/2 cup)
  • Salt (1/10 of a teaspoon)

Recipe instructions (learned from a friend)

  1. Warm a pan with oil on a stove.
  2. Clean and remove the cover of potatoes, cut them into slices.
  3. Add salt and fry until the potatoes become golden in 10-15 minutes of frying.
  4. Enjoy the fried potatoes.
