Steamed Broccoli

A very simple dish made of broccoli.

It is very healthy.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli leaves (10 pieces)
  • Clean water(1 cup)
  • Salt (1/4 of a teaspoon)

Recipe instructions (learned from a friend)

  1. Rinse the broccloi leaves.
  2. Pour clean water into a microwaveable dish with 1 cm deapness of water!
  3. Put broccoli leaves into the dish with water.
  4. Add salt into the dish with water and broccoli.
  5. Cover the top of the dish with a wet paper towel list so it covers the top of the dish .
  6. Put the dish into the microwave and microwave it for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Take out the dish out of the microwave and let it stay for 3-5 minutes.
  8. Open the paper paper towel cover and enjoy the steamed broccoli.
