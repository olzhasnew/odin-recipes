Fried eggs

A very simple dish made of eggs.

It is very nutricious.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (2 or more eggs depending on your needs)
  • Oil(1/2 cup)
  • Salt (1/10 of a teaspoon)

Recipe instructions (learned from a friend)

  1. Warm a pan with oil on a stove.
  2. Break the shells of eggs and pour the eggs' liquids onto the hot pan
  3. Add salt and fry until the eggs become solid in 3-5 minutes of frying.
  4. Enjoy the fried eggs.
